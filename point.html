    • What are Points?

    Points are a reward system that we offer to our volunteers as a token of appreciation for their contributions to our clean-up programs.

    How to Earn Points

    Volunteers can earn points by participating in various clean-up events,recycling plastics, and other activities organized by our organization. The more you volunteer, the more points you can earn!

    Redeeming Points

    Accumulated points can be redeemed for a variety of rewards, including eco-friendly merchandise, discounts on workshops, and even gift cards.

    Points Balance

    You can check your current points balance by logging into your account on our website. Your points balance will be updated after each event you participate in.
